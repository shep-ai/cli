=== shep workflow schedule --help ===
Usage: shep workflow schedule [options] <name>

Set a cron schedule on a workflow

Arguments:
  name                     Workflow name or ID

Options:
  -c, --cron <expression>  Cron expression (e.g., "0 9 * * MON" for every Monday
                           at 9am)
  -z, --timezone <tz>      IANA timezone (e.g., "America/New_York")
  --remove                 Remove the current schedule
  -r, --repo <path>        Repository path (defaults to current directory)
  -h, --help               display help for command

Examples:
  shep workflow schedule my-workflow --cron "0 9 * * MON"           Every Monday at 9am
  shep workflow schedule my-workflow --cron "0 */6 * * *"           Every 6 hours
  shep workflow schedule my-workflow --cron "30 2 * * *" -z UTC     Daily at 2:30 AM UTC
  shep workflow schedule my-workflow --remove                        Remove schedule

Cron format: minute hour day-of-month month day-of-week
  minute:        0-59
  hour:          0-23
  day-of-month:  1-31
  month:         1-12 (or JAN-DEC)
  day-of-week:   0-7 (0 and 7 are Sunday, or MON-SUN)
